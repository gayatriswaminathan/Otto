The shift

Programs don't slip on engineering. They slip on coordination.

For a decade, technical program management has meant a senior human chasing status, reconciling dependencies, and rebuilding the same launch checklist by hand, every week, for every program. It doesn't scale, and the programs that can't get a TPM go uncovered.

Otto does that work autonomously. It reads your trackers and channels, keeps a live model of every program, catches the slip before the standup, and writes the update for you, escalating only the judgment calls a TPM would take to the owner anyway.
